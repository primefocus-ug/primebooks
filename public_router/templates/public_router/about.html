About PrimeBooks

Empowering Ugandan businesses with modern, EFRIS-compliant business management solutions that are locally supported and globally competitive.

Our Story

PrimeBooks was born from a deep understanding of the unique challenges faced by Ugandan businesses. We recognized that local businesses needed more than just accounting software – they needed a comprehensive business management solution that understands the Ugandan market, complies with local regulations, and provides the support businesses need to thrive.

Based in Nakawa, Kampala, we're more than just a software provider – we're your local partner in business success. Our team combines international software development expertise with deep knowledge of Uganda's business landscape to deliver a solution that truly works for you.

We built PrimeBooks to be EFRIS compliant from the ground up, ensuring that our clients can focus on growing their business while we handle the complexities of tax compliance and reporting. Our commitment to local support means you always have someone who understands your needs, speaks your language, and is just a phone call away.
