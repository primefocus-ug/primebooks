    Normal Invoices Help
    What are Normal Invoices?

    Normal invoices are valid invoices that:

    • Have been successfully uploaded to EFRIS
    • Are not obsolete/invalid
    • Do not have pending credit/debit note applications
    • Can be used to create credit notes
    Creating Credit Notes:

    Click the "Credit Note" button to cancel/refund an entire invoice. The system will:

    • Retrieve the original invoice details
    • Create negative amounts for all items
    • Submit the credit note application to EFRIS
    What Happens After?

    After submitting, you'll receive a reference number. EFRIS will review and approve/reject the application.
