Normal Invoices Help
What are Normal Invoices?

Normal invoices are valid invoices that:

  • Have been successfully uploaded to EFRIS
  • Are not obsolete/invalid
  • Do not have pending credit/debit note applications
  • Can be used to create credit or debit notes
Creating Credit/Debit Notes:

Click the "Credit Note" button next to an invoice to initiate a credit note application for that invoice.

Filtering:
  • Use Buyer TIN to find invoices for specific customers
  • Use date range to narrow down results
  • Leave filters empty to see all eligible invoices
