What is Customs UoM?

Customs Unit of Measure (UoM) is required by Uganda Revenue Authority for export documentation.

Why is this needed?
  • Mandatory for export invoices (v23.4+)
  • Different from regular sales UoM
  • Used in T130 product registration
  • Must be from T115 exportRateUnit list
Important:
  • Configure BEFORE creating export invoices
  • Cannot export without this
  • Sent to EFRIS via T130
Examples:
  • Coffee: NTT (Net Metric Tons)
  • Juice: LTR (Litres)
  • Cotton: KGM (Kilograms)
  • Fabric: MTK (Square Meters)
