About USSD Access

USSD (Unstructured Supplementary Service Data) allows taxpayers to access EFRIS services via basic mobile phones without internet connection.

Features:
  • Issue invoices/receipts from any mobile phone
  • No smartphone or internet required
  • Works on all mobile networks in Uganda
  • Simple menu-driven interface
Requirements:
  • Valid TIN registered with URA
  • Active mobile number registered to taxpayer
  • EFRIS account must be active
Note: After account creation, the taxpayer will receive SMS instructions for accessing the USSD service.
