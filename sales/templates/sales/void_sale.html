Void Guidelines
When to Void a Sale:
  • Customer requests cancellation immediately
  • Pricing errors discovered before fulfillment
  • Duplicate transactions identified
  • System errors affecting transaction accuracy
  • Fraud prevention measures
Important Reminders:
  • Voids are different from refunds
  • Sales with existing refunds cannot be voided
  • All void actions are permanently logged
  • Consider using refunds for completed transactions
Restrictions:
  • Sales older than 7 days cannot be voided
  • Only regular sales can be voided
  • Refunded sales cannot be voided
  • Already voided sales cannot be re-voided
