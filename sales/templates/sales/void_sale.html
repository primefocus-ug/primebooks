Void Guidelines
When to Void a Sale:
  • Customer requests cancellation immediately
  • Pricing errors discovered before fulfillment
  • Duplicate transactions identified
  • System errors affecting transaction accuracy
  • Fraud prevention measures
Product vs Service Handling:
  • Products: Stock will be restored to inventory
  • Services: Will be voided and logged (no stock restoration)
  • Both types will appear in audit logs
Important Reminders:
  • Voids are different from refunds
  • Sales with existing refunds cannot be voided
  • All void actions are permanently logged
  • Consider using refunds for completed transactions
Restrictions:
  • Sales older than 7 days cannot be voided
  • Only regular sales can be voided
  • Refunded sales cannot be voided
  • Already voided sales cannot be re-voided
Audit Trail

What gets recorded:

  • User who performed the void
  • Date and time of void action
  • Void reason and detailed notes
  • Original sale details
  • Items and quantities affected (products & services)
  • Payment information
  • Stock movements (for products)

Access Requirements:

  • Proper user permissions
  • Access to the sale's store
  • Detailed reason documentation
