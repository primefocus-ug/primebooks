Refund Processing Guidelines

Processing a refund will create a refund transaction. Product stock will be restored, but services cannot be returned to inventory.

  • Products: Selected items will be returned to stock
  • Services: Will be refunded and logged (no stock restoration)
  • Refund payment will be processed via selected method
  • All refund actions are logged for audit purposes
  • Customer will be notified of the refund processing

Refund Guidelines
Refund Process:
  • Select items and quantities to refund
  • Provide a clear refund reason
  • Products will be returned to inventory
  • Services will be refunded and logged
  • Refund payment will be processed
  • Customer notification will be sent
Product vs Service Handling:
  • Products: Stock automatically restored
  • Services: Refunded but no stock impact
  • Both create audit trail entries
  • Both affect financial reports
Important Notes:
  • Partial refunds are allowed
  • Multiple refunds can be processed
  • All refunds are tracked and audited
  • Stock levels update immediately for products
Restrictions:
  • Cannot refund voided sales
  • Cannot exceed available quantities
  • Refund reason is mandatory
  • Some payment methods may have delays
